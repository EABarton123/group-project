HEALTHY AND SUSTAINABLE RECIPES

A bit about what we do.

Here at Karington Recipes we want to share healthy environment friendly recipes. All our recipes use organic ingredients that can be grown in the UK. We adapt our website for the seasons, so depending on what time of year it is all our recipes will be seasonal. Some items can be foraged (although this needs to be done with expert knowledge) and we encourage anyone who can to home grow as many ingredients as possible.

Who are we?

We are three food mad friends who have joined to share our favourite recipes. However with Climate change, food wastage, and international travel on the rise we want to go back to basics. We show that food can be nutricious, sustainable and not cost the earth. We hope you enjoy!
